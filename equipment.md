## Maintenance reminders

- **Re-string:** every 1–3 months depending on play time. Sweaty hands = sooner.
- **Wipe down strings after every session** with a dry cloth — extends string life noticeably.
- **Truss rod / setup:** if action feels high or frets buzz, take to a tech (don't DIY truss rod adjustments yet).
- **Humidity:** electric guitars are more forgiving than acoustics, but if it's very dry in winter, a guitar humidifier is cheap insurance.
