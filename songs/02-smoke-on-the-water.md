## 🎸 Guitar and tone

### Which guitar to use?

Both work — Smoke on the Water is usually played on a **Les Paul / humbucker**, but the Tele's bridge humbucker (SH) or the Strat's bridge humbucker (HSS) sound excellent.

- **Tele SH** (bridge humbucker, position 1): a bit warmer and denser — a great option
- **Strat HSS** (bridge humbucker, position 1): a bit more aggressive — also great

### Yamaha THR5 — settings

| Parameter | Setting |
|---|---|
| Amp Type | CRUNCH |
| Gain | 5–6 |
| Master | 4–5 |
| Bass | 6 |
| Mid | 6 |
| Treble | 5 |
| Reverb | Very low (1) or off |

### BOSS SD-1

- Optional: adds sustain and grit. If you use it: Drive ~3, Level ~6.
- The CRUNCH channel at gain 5–6 is already close to the original tone.
