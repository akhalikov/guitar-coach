## Estimated Total Duration

At 5–6 days/week, 30–45 min/session:
- **Part 1 (Phases 1–15):** ~8–10 months
- **Part 2 (Phases 16–18):** ~2–3 months
- **Part 3 (alongside everything):** continuous

**Total: ~10–13 months to complete Vol 1 confidently.** Don't optimize for speed. The slow path is the path.
