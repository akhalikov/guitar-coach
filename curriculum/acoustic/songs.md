## Lane rules (quick reference)

Same rules as electric — see `../../prompts/base.md` → "Repertoire lanes" for the full version:

- One song in Learning at a time (allow two for ~1 week, then move the older one to Polishing).
- Short session (<20 min): pull from Learning first.
- Low-energy / bad-day mode: pull from Maintenance first.
- Promote Learning → Polishing when playable end-to-end without restarting.
- Promote Polishing → Maintenance when clean at tempo, from memory.
